Chairman's Message

Founded in 2007 with a imparting quality technical education, Saraswati Industrial training Institute (S.I.T.I.) has truly come a long way in establishing itself in the field of technical education in divers streams.

Our principal goal remains to establish SITI as a pioneer industrial training institute competitive with the best institutes nationally and next to none in the state and region. To achieve this goal, we endeavor to provide our students with the best of all kinds of facilities lab equipments, excellent infrastructure, facilities of extra curricular and co-curricular activities and the support of committed and competent faculty.

I congratulate and welcome all those prospective students who have decided to join us. At SITI you can look forward to a value-added career in the field of technical education.

With Best Wishes.
Rakesh Kumar Saini
