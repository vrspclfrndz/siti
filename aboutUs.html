About Us
Saraswati ITI is established by United Marines Engg. Educational Social Welfare & Charitable Society, Kurukshetra.

Our Mission

To provide the technical education environment in such a way that prepares our students for successful professional careers. Address the changing need of the industry. Responds to the technological challenged facing the state and the nation.

Our vision

We aspire to make Saraswati ITI one of the state's leading Industrial Training Institute, recognized for the excellence in technical education. We seek to be preferred choice of students seeking technical education.
